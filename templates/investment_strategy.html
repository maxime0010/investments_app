Investment Strategy

Our approach to building a high-performing portfolio involves a rigorous and data-driven process:

1. Data Collection

We gather price targets from top analysts covering major U.S. stocks in the S&P 500.

2. Predictive Analysis

We predict future stock prices using:

  • Frequency of Updates: Ensuring our model reflects the latest market intelligence.
  • Analyst Success Rate: Factoring in past performance to assess accuracy.
  • Analyst Consensus: Valuing predictions supported by multiple analysts.

3. Daily Market Screening

Our model is updated daily to optimize portfolio returns, ensuring we stay ahead of market trends.
